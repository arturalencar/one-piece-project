Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, also known as "Straw Hat" is the founder and captain of the increasingly infamous and powerful Straw Hat Pirates, as well as the most powerful of their best fighters. He wants to find the legendary treasure left behind by the late Gol D. Roger and thus become the Pirate King.

Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro, also known as "Pirate Hunter" Zoro, is the fighter of the Straw Hat Pirates and a former bounty hunter. He is one of the four best fighters in the crew and his dream is to become the greatest swordsman in the world.
